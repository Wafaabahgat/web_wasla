about us

Wasla consists of a group of companies that collectively provide smart and sustainable urban mobility solutions. Backed by investments and support from the National Bank of Egypt (NBE), our solution components range from operations management to fleet maintenance, advertising and commercial asset management. All are based on a robust set of world-renowned transport technologies that have been adapted and developed for the local context.

We aim to destigmatize public transportation in Egypt and Africa by providing reliable, world-class mobility services tailored to complex metropolises in emerging markets.

Backed by more than 1000 enthusiastic team members working in our companies, Wasla Group is fully equipped to handle the current infrastructure developments in Egypt and shoulder the responsibility of being the partner of choice to achieve success in mega transportation projects.
